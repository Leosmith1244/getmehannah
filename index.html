About Hannah

Hannah brings a calm, polished, get-it-done approach to live events. With experience supporting weddings, day-of event execution, VIP meet and greets, hospitality operations, and high-pressure environments, she helps events stay organized, on time, and guest-ready.

Her style is warm, professional, and efficient. She works well with vendors, hosts, guests, and planners, and knows how to step in where needed so the person hosting the event can actually enjoy it.
